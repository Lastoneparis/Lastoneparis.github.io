Cocotte — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Cocotte is a recipe keeper designed to work entirely on your device.

Data we collect

Cocotte collects no personal data. Your recipes, photos, shopping lists, timers and settings are stored only on your device (and your device's standard backups). We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising.

Importing recipes from the web

When you import a recipe from a URL, your device fetches that page directly from the website you chose — exactly like a browser would. Cocotte sends nothing to us or to anyone else, and only the recipe you save is kept, locally.

Notifications

Cooking timers use local notifications scheduled on your device. Nothing leaves your phone.

Purchases

Cocotte Premium subscriptions and the lifetime purchase are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled in your Apple ID settings at least 24 hours before the end of the period.

Your rights

Since we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to export or delete — deleting the app removes all local data.

Terms of Use

Cocotte uses Apple's standard Licensed Application End User License Agreement (EULA).
