Cocotte — Support

Cocotte keeps every recipe you love in one private cookbook: import from the web, cook step by step with timers, and build your shopping list in one tap.

FAQ

How do I import a recipe from a website?

Tap + in the Recipes tab, choose Import from web, and paste the page's link. Cocotte reads the recipe straight from the page. If a site isn't recognised, you can paste the recipe text instead — ingredients and steps are detected automatically.

Why does the screen stay on while cooking?

That's Cook Mode: while you follow a recipe step by step, Cocotte keeps the screen awake so you never have to touch your phone with floury hands.

Where are my recipes stored?

Only on your device. Cocotte has no accounts and no cloud — see the Privacy Policy.

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

Open Settings → Cocotte Premium → Restore purchases on any device signed in with the same Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple: iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Cocotte.
