AdScope — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

AdScope is a dashboard for your App Store keyword rankings and Apple Search Ads campaigns. It is built to keep your data on your device.

Data we collect

AdScope collects no personal data and has no servers, accounts or analytics. Your tracked apps, keywords, ranking history and settings are stored only on your device (and your device's standard backups).

Apple Search Ads credentials

If you connect your Apple Search Ads account, your API credentials (client ID, team ID, key ID and private key) are stored only in your device's Keychain and are used exclusively to call Apple's Search Ads API directly from your device. They are never transmitted to us or any third party.

Network requests

AdScope talks to two Apple services only: the public iTunes Search API (keyword rankings, app metadata) and the Apple Search Ads API (your campaigns, if connected). These requests are covered by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Purchases

Subscriptions and the lifetime purchase are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled in your Apple ID settings at least 24 hours before the period ends.

Your rights

We hold no data about you. Deleting the app removes all local data, including Keychain credentials.

Terms of Use

AdScope uses Apple's standard Licensed Application End User License Agreement (EULA).
