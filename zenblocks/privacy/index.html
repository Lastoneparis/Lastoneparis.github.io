ZenBlocks — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

ZenBlocks is designed to be played entirely offline. We built it to collect as little data as possible.

Data we collect

ZenBlocks itself collects no personal data. Your scores, streaks, token balance and settings are stored only on your device (and your device's standard backups). We have no servers, no accounts, and no access to your data.

Advertising

The free version of ZenBlocks shows ads served by Google AdMob. When ads are shown, Google may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's data practices. On iOS, you will be asked for tracking permission (App Tracking Transparency); if you decline, ads are non-personalized. Where required (e.g. EEA/UK), a consent form is shown before any ad is served.

Purchasing Remove Ads Forever removes all advertising, after which no ad-related data is processed at all. Ads are also never shown while your device is offline.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.

Children

ZenBlocks does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ad serving follows Google's policies for family-friendly treatment where applicable.

Your rights

Since we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to export or delete — deleting the app removes all local data. For ad-related data, see Google's privacy controls at adssettings.google.com.

Terms of Use

ZenBlocks uses Apple's standard Licensed Application End User License Agreement (EULA).
