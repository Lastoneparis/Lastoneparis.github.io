Capzo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Capzo is a screenshot and screen-capture studio for iOS and macOS. Everything it makes stays on your device.

Data we collect

Capzo collects no personal data. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics and no ads. Your screenshots, recordings and projects are processed and stored entirely on your device.

Screen recording and screenshots (macOS)

Capzo asks for macOS screen-recording permission to capture your screen, and optionally Accessibility permission to detect mouse clicks so it can apply smooth zoom effects. Captures and click data are used only to render your export and never leave your Mac.

Photos (iOS)

Capzo asks for photo library access only to import the screenshots you pick and to save your finished exports. It never scans or uploads your library.

Purchases

Subscriptions and the lifetime purchase are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled in your Apple ID settings at least 24 hours before the period ends.

Your rights

We hold no data about you. Deleting the app removes all its local data.

Terms of Use

Capzo uses Apple's standard Licensed Application End User License Agreement (EULA).
