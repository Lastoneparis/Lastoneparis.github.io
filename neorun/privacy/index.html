Neorun 3D — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Neorun 3D is an endless runner game designed to be played anywhere, offline.

Data we collect

Neorun 3D itself collects no personal data. Your scores, prisms, unlocked skins and settings are stored only on your device (and your device's standard backups). We have no servers and no accounts.

Advertising

The free version of Neorun 3D may show ads served by Google AdMob. When ads are shown, Google may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's data practices. On iOS you will be asked for tracking permission (App Tracking Transparency); if you decline, ads are non-personalized. Purchasing Remove Ads Forever removes all advertising, after which no ad-related data is processed. Ads are never shown while your device is offline.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details.

Children

Neorun 3D does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ad serving follows Google's policies where applicable.

Your rights

We hold no data about you. For ad-related choices, see adssettings.google.com. Deleting the app removes all local data.

Terms of Use

Neorun 3D uses Apple's standard Licensed Application End User License Agreement (EULA).
