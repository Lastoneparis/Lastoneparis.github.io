Vallon — Support

Vallon is a relaxing farming sim you can play entirely offline: till, plant, water, harvest, and grow your own cozy little valley through four seasons.

Frequently asked questions

Is my farm saved automatically?

Yes. Your farm auto-saves every night when you sleep and whenever the app goes to the background. Deleting the app deletes the save file.

What does Premium include?

Your whole first season (spring) is free. Vallon Premium unlocks summer, autumn and winter, the farm expansion, and the rare crown crops. It is available as a monthly or yearly subscription, or a one-time lifetime purchase.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open the Premium screen (Settings → Vallon Premium) and tap Restore Purchases, signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple: Settings app → your name → Subscriptions → Vallon. Cancelling keeps Premium active until the end of the paid period.

Does Vallon need an internet connection?

No. The game is fully playable offline. A connection is only needed for App Store purchases and restores.
