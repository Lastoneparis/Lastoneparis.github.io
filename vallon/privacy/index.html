Vallon — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Vallon is a cozy farming game designed to work entirely on your device, fully offline.

Data we collect

Vallon collects no personal data. Your farm, progress and settings are stored only on your device (and your device's standard backups). We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and the game never connects to the internet to play.

Purchases

Vallon Premium subscriptions and the lifetime purchase are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled in your Apple ID settings at least 24 hours before the end of the period.

Your rights

Since we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to export or delete — deleting the app removes all local data, including your save file.

Children

Vallon shows no ads and collects no data, from anyone. Purchases are gated by the standard App Store payment flow.

Terms of Use

Vallon uses Apple's standard Licensed Application End User License Agreement (EULA).
