LumiStudio — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 7, 2026

LumiStudio is an AI photo editor that runs 100% on your device. Subject cutouts, studio backgrounds, object removal and batch editing are all powered by on-device Apple Vision technology.

Data we collect

LumiStudio collects no data at all. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising and no servers of ours. We never see your photos, your edits or anything about you.

Your photos

Photos you edit are processed entirely on your device and are never uploaded anywhere. LumiStudio only accesses the photos you explicitly select, and edited results are saved back to your photo library only when you choose to export them.

On-device AI

Subject detection, background removal and object erasing use Apple's Vision framework running locally on your iPhone or iPad. No image data leaves your device — LumiStudio works fully offline.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We never see or store your payment details. You can manage or restore purchases through your Apple ID at any time.

Your rights

Because we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to export or delete. Deleting the app removes all of its local data from your device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Terms of Use
