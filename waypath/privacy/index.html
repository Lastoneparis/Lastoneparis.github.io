Waypath — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Waypath is a run, walk and ride tracker. Your activities belong to you and stay on your device.

Data we collect

Waypath collects no personal data. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics and no ads. Your activities, GPS tracks, routes and heatmap are stored only on your device (and your device's standard backups).

Location

Waypath uses your location only to record the activities you start and to show your position on the map. Background location is active only while you are recording an activity, and recording stops when you stop it. Location data is never transmitted anywhere — the heatmap is computed entirely on your device from your own activities.

Purchases

Subscriptions and the lifetime purchase are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled in your Apple ID settings at least 24 hours before the period ends.

Your rights

We hold no data about you. You can delete any activity in the app, and deleting the app removes all local data.

Terms of Use

Waypath uses Apple's standard Licensed Application End User License Agreement (EULA).
