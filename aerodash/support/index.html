AeroDash — Support

AeroDash is a fast neon arcade dodger that works 100% offline — perfect for flights, subway rides and dead zones. Drag one finger, skim past obstacles for near-miss combos, and chase your best score.

FAQ

Does the game need an internet connection?

No. AeroDash is fully playable offline — on a plane, in the subway or with no signal. The only feature that uses the internet is the in-app Shop, so purchases can be processed by the App Store.

How do I earn and spend tokens?

You earn tokens just by playing and from your daily streak. Spend them to continue a run after a crash or to unlock new neon ships. You can also buy token packs in the Shop.

What does "Remove Ads" do?

It's a one-time purchase that permanently removes ads. There are no subscriptions in AeroDash.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open Settings → Restore Purchases on any device signed in with the same Apple ID. Your "Remove Ads" purchase will be restored.

I lost my progress / changed device.

Progress (best score, tokens, ships, streak) is stored on your device and in its standard backups. Restoring your device from a backup restores your progress; there are no accounts or cloud saves.

The Shop says it's unavailable.

The Shop needs a connection to load prices from the App Store. The game itself keeps working offline — connect to the internet and reopen the Shop to purchase.
