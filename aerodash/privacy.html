AeroDash — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

AeroDash is a neon arcade game designed to work entirely on your device, even in airplane mode.

Data we collect

AeroDash collects no personal data. Your best score, tokens, unlocked ships, daily streak and settings are stored only on your device (and your device's standard backups). We have no servers, no accounts, no sign-up, no analytics, and no tracking.

Playing offline

The game is fully playable with no internet connection. Nothing about how you play leaves your phone.

Purchases

The optional "Remove Ads" purchase and token packs are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. These are one-time purchases — AeroDash has no subscriptions.

Advertising

AeroDash may show occasional ads to players who have not bought "Remove Ads". The current release does not integrate any third-party advertising SDK and does not use the device advertising identifier (IDFA). If that ever changes, this policy and the App Store privacy label will be updated before the feature ships.

Children

AeroDash does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

Your rights

Since we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to export or delete — deleting the app removes all local data.

Terms of Use

AeroDash uses Apple's standard Licensed Application End User License Agreement (EULA).
