Aurora — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Aurora is a music alarm clock designed to work entirely on your device.

Data we collect

Aurora collects no personal data. Your alarms, themes and settings are stored only on your device (and your device's standard backups). We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising.

Apple Music

If you choose Apple Music as a wake-up source, Aurora asks for permission to access Apple Music via Apple's MusicKit. Playback and your library are handled by Apple under Apple's Privacy Policy; Aurora never transmits your listening data anywhere.

Notifications

Alarms use local notifications scheduled on your device. Nothing leaves your phone.

Purchases

Aurora Premium subscriptions and the lifetime purchase are processed entirely by Apple. We never see your payment details. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled in your Apple ID settings at least 24 hours before the end of the period.

Your rights

Since we hold no data about you, there is nothing for us to export or delete — deleting the app removes all local data.

Terms of Use

Aurora uses Apple's standard Licensed Application End User License Agreement (EULA).
